32. After-Hours Leads: The 63% You're Throwing Away

April 01, 2026 3 min read By Call Boss Team

Overview

Here's a number that should concern you: 63% of legal inquiries happen outside traditional business hours. Between 6 PM and 8 AM. On weekends. On holidays. When your office is dark and your phones are forwarded to an answering service that takes messages nobody reads until Monday. 63% of your leads. When People Need Lawyers People don't have legal emergencies on a schedule. Car accidents happen at 11 PM. DUI arrests happen at 2 AM. Domestic disputes happen on Sunday mornings. The moment someone realizes they need an attorney is almost never during business hours. Even for non-emergency matters: people research attorneys at night. They Google "estate planning attorney" after the kids go to bed. They fill out a "free case evaluation" form during their lunch break. They text their friend for a lawyer recommendation at 8 PM. These are ready buyers. And they're hitting your voicemail. The Answering Service Band-Aid "But we have an answering service for after hours." Cool. What does your answering service actually do? They answer. They take a message. They send you an email. That's it. They don't: - Qualify the lead - Answer questions about your practice - Book appointments - Send follow-up texts - Provide reassurance or guidance - Warm-transfer to an attorney for urgent matters A message isn't an intake.
