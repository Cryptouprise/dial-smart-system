The MCA Industry's Biggest Waste Problem

April 01, 2026 3 min read By Call Boss Team

Overview

The MCA industry runs on volume. Dial more. Submit more. Fund more. The numbers game is real -- more activity generally equals more funded deals. But the numbers game has a dirty secret: it's incredibly wasteful. You buy 10,000 merchant leads. Your reps dial all 10,000. They fund 200. That's a 2% conversion rate. Industry standard. Nothing to be ashamed of. Except... 9,800 merchants just disappeared into your CRM. And you paid $25-$50 each for them. That's $245,000-$490,000 in lead cost that generated zero return. "That's just the cost of doing business," you say. And you've been saying it for years. What if it didn't have to be?

Where the 9,800 Actually Went

Let's break down what happened to those 9,800 unfunded leads: 5,000 - Never reached. Voicemail, wrong number, disconnected, business closed. These are genuinely low-value. But "never reached" doesn't mean "reached and not interested." There's a difference. 2,000 - Reached but not ready. "Not right now." "Call me back later." "I just got funded." These are TIMING issues, not interest issues. In 3-6 months, many of these merchants will need funding. 1,200 - Interested but didn't convert. Had conversations. Expressed interest. But the process stalled -- didn't complete application, didn't send statements, got a better offer, got scared of daily payments. 800 - Partially qualified. Revenue was borderline. Time in business was short. Industry was risky. They didn't qualify for your A-paper funders but might qualify for B-paper. 500 - Positive interactions that fell through cracks. Said "send me info," expressed interest in a text, had a good conversation that the rep didn't follow up on. The Golden Garbage. 300 - Didn't qualify but will. New businesses that are 6 months from having enough revenue history. Growing businesses that will cross your minimum thresholds soon.

How AI Works the 9,800

The 5,000 "Never Reached": AI runs a 30-day multi-channel outreach: calls at different times, texts, emails. AI has infinite patience for the timing game. Many of those "never reached" leads are simply people who don't answer unknown numbers but DO respond to texts. AI adapts. Expected recovery: 500-750 conversations generated (10-15% contact rate with persistent multi-channel outreach). The 2,000 "Not Right Now": AI adds to a 6-month nurture with monthly check-ins timed around business cycles. Quarterly refresh offers. Seasonal capital reminders. Expected recovery: 100-200 funded deals over 12 months (5-10% eventual conversion). The 1,200 "Interested But Stalled": AI restarts the application process. Sends the link again. Follows up on missing documents. Addresses the objection that stalled them. This is the highest-value segment. Expected recovery: 180-360 funded deals (15-30% conversion with proper follow-through). The 800 "Partially Qualified": AI maintains light contact and re-checks qualification quarterly. As revenue grows or as you add new funder relationships with different criteria, these leads become qualified. Expected recovery: 40-80 funded deals over 12 months. The 500 "Golden Garbage": AI immediately engages every positive signal. No positive interaction goes unfollowed. This is free money. Expected recovery: 75-150 funded deals (15-30% conversion). Total Recovery

From those 9,800 "wasted" leads: 425-1,600 additional funded deals over 12 months. Your reps funded 200 deals from those 10,000 leads. AI potentially doubles or triples that number from the same leads, with zero additional lead spend. The problem isn't your lead quality.
