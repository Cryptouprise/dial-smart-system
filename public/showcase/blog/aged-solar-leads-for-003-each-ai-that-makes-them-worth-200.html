Aged Solar Leads for $0.03 Each. AI That Makes Them Worth $200.

April 01, 2026 4 min read By Call Boss Team

The Aged Lead Goldmine

Everyone in solar knows about aged leads. They're the leads that didn't convert for other companies — maybe 30 days old, maybe 90, maybe a year. You can buy them for pennies on the dollar. Fresh lead: $60-120. 30-day aged lead: $5-15. 90-day aged lead: $1-5. Year-old lead: $0.03-0.50. Most solar companies look at those prices and think: "You get what you pay for." They assume aged leads are garbage — wrong numbers, tire kickers, people who already went solar with someone else. Some of them are. But not all of them. Not even close.

Why Aged Leads Are Undervalued

Here's what most people miss about aged leads: They've already raised their hand. At some point, this person was interested enough in solar to fill out a form, click an ad, or take a call. That interest doesn't evaporate — it goes dormant. They were poorly followed up. The previous company that bought this lead probably called once or twice and gave up. Sound familiar? If you have the same follow-up problem, yeah, they'll be garbage for you too. But with AI... Circumstances change. The person who couldn't afford solar 6 months ago may have gotten a raise, refinanced their home, or gotten tired of $300 electric bills. The person whose spouse said no may have won them over after watching their neighbor save money. The competition gave up. Nobody else is calling these people anymore. You have the field completely to yourself. No speed-to-lead race. No competing offers. Just you and a homeowner who once expressed interest.

The AI Approach to Aged Leads

The reason aged leads have a bad reputation is that people work them the same way they work fresh leads — manual calls with a dialer. That doesn't work because: 1. The connect rate is lower (numbers change, people screen calls) 2. The context is different (they don't remember the inquiry) 3. The volume is too high for human follow-up 4. The conversion rate per individual lead is lower (but the cost is SO low it doesn't matter) AI solves all of these: Volume: AI can work 100,000 aged leads in a week. No human team can touch that. Multi-channel: AI doesn't just call. It texts: "Hey [Name], we reached out a while back about solar for [Address]. Electricity rates in [City] went up 12% since then — want to see updated savings?" Text response rates on aged leads are actually higher than call connect rates. Persistence: AI follows up 8-12 times over 30 days. The 3% that respond to the first text become 8-10% after the full sequence. Cost-per-attempt: $0.02-0.05 per contact attempt. At those economics, even a 1% conversion rate is wildly profitable.

The Economics That Make Your Head Spin

Let's buy 100,000 aged leads at $0.03 each. - Lead cost: $3,000 - AI outreach cost (12 touches x $0.03 per touch): $36,000 - Total campaign cost: $39,000 Results (conservative): - Text response rate: 6% = 6,000 conversations - Appointment rate from conversations: 12% = 720 appointments - Show rate (with AI confirmation): 70% = 504 sits - Close rate: 15% (lower for aged) = 76 deals - Average deal value: $28,000 - Revenue: $2,128,000 $39,000 in → $2,128,000 out. That's 54X ROI. Even if my numbers are off by half — even if you only close 38 deals — that's still over a million dollars from a $39,000 investment. Now compare that to fresh leads: - 100,000 fresh leads at $80 = $8,000,000 - You can't even afford to try. With aged leads, you can experiment for $39K. The downside is losing $39K. The upside is $2M+.

The Aged Lead Playbook

Step 1: Buy smart Focus on leads from your specific market area. Verify that the data includes name, phone, address, and ideally previous interest indicator. Step 2: Clean the list Run the numbers through a phone validation service. Remove disconnected numbers, landlines (for text campaigns), and duplicate entries. This might cost $0.01-0.02 per record. Step 3: AI text campaign first Start with text outreach — it's cheaper than calls and has higher response rates on aged data: "Hi [Name], this is [Company] in [City]. We were looking at homes in your area for solar and yours looks like a great fit. Electric rates went up again — want to see what solar would save you?" Step 4: AI call campaign for responders + non-responders People who respond positively get a call from AI to qualify and book. People who don't respond to texts get called. Step 5: Full follow-up sequence Every lead gets the full 8-12 touch treatment. No lead gets abandoned after one attempt. Step 6: Recurring sweeps Run the aged list again every 90 days. People who didn't respond in January might respond in April.

The Secret Nobody Tells You About Aged Leads

Here's the thing: aged leads only work if you have the infrastructure to work them properly. If you're going to call 100,000 aged leads one time each with a human dialer team, you'll get garbage results and confirm your bias that aged leads are worthless. But if you have an AI system that can: - Text and call 100,000 people across 30 days - Handle thousands of simultaneous conversations - Follow up 12 times on every lead - Qualify and book without human involvement ...then aged leads become the most profitable channel in your business. The lead isn't the variable. The follow-up system is. $0.03 leads. $200 appointment value. The margin is insane — if you have the machine to extract it. We built that machine.
