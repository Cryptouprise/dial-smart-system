// The Verdict

Every Dialer Has Its Place.

They built tools. We built a brain.

VICIdial

"Free"*

Open-source predictive dialer. The software is free, but you still pay for telephony, hosting, SIP trunks, server admins, and weeks of setup. No AI, no learning, no follow-up automation.

BEST FOR: DIY teams with server admins

Five9

$150+/seat + minutes

Enterprise contact center. Great for 100+ seat operations with dedicated training budgets. Expensive, rigid, requires months of onboarding.

BEST FOR: Large enterprise call centers

GoHighLevel

$97-497/mo

Marketing CRM with a power dialer bolted on. Great for what happens after the dialer — CRM, funnels, automation. Has dialer features, but not built for scale.

BEST FOR: Agencies wanting all-in-one CRM

Call Boss

$0.15-0.25/min + add-ons

AI-first autonomous dialing system. Calls, books, follows up, and learns with zero human intervention. Built for teams that want results, not a system to manage.

BEST FOR: Teams that want AI to do the work
