Engine 06
SMS A/B
Testing

Every text message is an experiment. The UCB1 bandit algorithm continuously shifts traffic toward winners, rewrites losers using AI, and tracks what actually moves leads forward — not just open rates.

UCB1 bandit algorithm — mathematically optimal traffic splitting. Not 50/50 guessing.
Tracks real outcomes — reply rate, positive sentiment, appointments booked, opt-outs
AI rewrites losers — variants with <5% reply rate get automatically rewritten by the AI
Max 4 variants — focused testing, not scattered. One clear winner per context.
Context-aware — different variants for different lead stages, industries, and dispositions
