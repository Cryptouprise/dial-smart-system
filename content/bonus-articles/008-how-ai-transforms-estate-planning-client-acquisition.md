# 8. How AI Transforms Estate Planning Client Acquisition

Estate planning has the longest sales cycle in all of legal. And that's exactly why AI follow-up is a goldmine.

Think about the typical estate planning lead. They Google "estate planning attorney" after their neighbor mentions it at a barbecue. They fill out your form, get your auto-response, and then... life happens. The kids have soccer. Work is busy. The urgency fades.

Three months later, they Google it again. Fill out someone else's form.

You had them. You lost them. Because you followed up once, maybe twice, and then moved on.

**The "I'll Get To It" Problem**

Estate planning isn't urgent — until it is. And by the time it's urgent (health crisis, family death), people aren't shopping. They're panicking.

The opportunity is in the non-urgent phase. When people are thinking about it, know they should do it, but keep pushing it off. These people need consistent, gentle reminders that estate planning exists and matters.

AI excels here. A 6-month follow-up sequence that checks in monthly: "Hi David, just following up on your estate planning inquiry. Many of our clients find that setting up a trust takes less time than they expected — usually just one or two meetings. Would you like to schedule a quick 15-minute call to discuss your options?"

No human is doing that for 200 leads. AI does it for 2,000.

**The Consultation Booking**

Estate planning consultations need to be easy to book. AI integrates with your calendar and can schedule appointments in real-time. "I have openings this Thursday at 2 PM and Friday at 10 AM. Which works better for you?" No back-and-forth phone tag. No "call us during business hours."

**The Family Conversation**

Estate planning often involves spouses making a decision together. AI can communicate with both parties, send information to both email addresses, and ensure both are scheduled for the consultation. It handles the logistics that otherwise require 3-4 phone calls.

**The Document Preparation**

Before the consultation, you need information: asset overview, family structure, existing documents, goals. AI gathers this through a conversational intake process that feels natural, not like filling out a 10-page questionnaire.

By the time the attorney sits down for the consultation, they have a complete picture. The consultation is productive instead of administrative. Clients feel their time was respected. They sign.

**The Upsell and Maintenance**

Estate plans need updating. Life changes, laws change. AI can re-engage past clients: "Hi Susan, it's been two years since we completed your estate plan. Since then, the estate tax exemption has changed. Would you like to schedule a review?"

That's recurring revenue on autopilot. No attorney remembers to call 500 past clients every two years. AI does.

**Revenue Case**

Average estate plan: $2,500-$5,000 for a trust-based plan. If you capture 5 extra clients per month through better follow-up, that's $12,500-$25,000 in monthly revenue. Plus the annual reviews, updates, and referrals from satisfied clients.

Estate planning is a long game. AI is the only player patient enough to play it properly.
