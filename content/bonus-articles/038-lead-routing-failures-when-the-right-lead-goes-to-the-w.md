# 38. Lead Routing Failures: When the Right Lead Goes to the Wrong Place

Your personal injury lead just got routed to your estate planning attorney. Your immigration lead is sitting in the general inbox because nobody knows who handles those. Your high-value commercial litigation inquiry went to voicemail because the person responsible for those leads is in court.

Welcome to lead routing at most law firms. It's chaos.

**The Routing Problem**

Multi-practice firms have a nightmare on their hands: different practice areas need different intake processes, different qualifying questions, and different attorneys. A system that works for PI doesn't work for criminal defense doesn't work for family law.

Most firms handle this with a receptionist who "knows" where to route calls. When that receptionist is out? When a new receptionist starts? When call volume spikes? The routing breaks down.

Leads go to voicemail. Leads go to the wrong person. Leads sit in a shared inbox and nobody takes ownership. High-value leads get treated the same as tire-kickers.

**What Smart Routing Looks Like**

AI qualifies the lead FIRST, then routes based on:

- **Practice area:** Determined through the intake conversation, not a menu tree
- **Lead quality:** High-value leads get priority routing
- **Attorney availability:** Routes to whoever is actually available, not whoever's "supposed" to handle it
- **Urgency:** Emergency matters (custody, arrest, levy) get escalated immediately
- **Geography:** For firms with multiple offices, routes to the nearest location
- **Language:** Routes to bilingual staff when needed

The lead doesn't need to know any of this. They have a natural conversation, answer some questions, and the system handles the rest.

**The Priority Queue**

Not all leads are created equal. A $100,000 personal injury case shouldn't get the same treatment as a $500 traffic ticket. AI scores leads based on qualification answers and routes accordingly.

High-value leads get immediate warm transfer to a senior attorney. Medium-value leads get scheduled for the next available consultation. Low-value leads get information and follow-up sequences.

**The Result**

Every lead reaches the right person, at the right time, with the right information. No misdirects. No lost leads. No "I thought you were handling that" conversations.

Lead routing shouldn't require a human traffic controller. It requires a system. AI is that system.
