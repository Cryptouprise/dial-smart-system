# 28. No-Show Rates: The Silent Revenue Killer

Let me tell you the most depressing number in legal practice: the average consultation no-show rate is 30-40%.

You spent $200 to acquire the lead. You or your intake person spent 15 minutes qualifying them. You booked a consultation. The attorney blocked out 30 minutes. And the person just... didn't show up.

Multiply that by 10-15 no-shows per month and you're looking at thousands of dollars in wasted attorney time and marketing spend.

**Why People No-Show**

It's usually one of these:

1. **They forgot.** Life happened. The appointment was Thursday at 2 PM and they just forgot.
2. **They got cold feet.** The urgency faded between booking and the appointment.
3. **They hired someone else.** A faster-responding firm got to them in the interim.
4. **The logistics were wrong.** They didn't know where to go, what to bring, or what to expect.

Every single one of these is preventable.

**The AI Reminder Machine**

Our system sends automated reminders that aren't just "don't forget your appointment." They're strategic re-engagement:

- **24 hours before:** "Hi Sarah, just a reminder about your consultation tomorrow at 2 PM with Attorney Johnson. Here's the office address and parking info. Is there anything you'd like to prepare?"

- **2 hours before:** "Looking forward to speaking with you in a couple hours. If you need to reschedule, just reply to this text."

- **30 minutes before:** "We're ready for you! If you're running late, no worries — just let us know."

- **15 minutes AFTER no-show:** "Hi Sarah, it looks like we missed you today. No problem at all — would you like to reschedule? I have openings tomorrow and Friday."

That last one is the money text. Most firms mark a no-show as a lost lead. We re-engage them within 15 minutes while the appointment is still fresh in their mind.

**The No-Show Recovery Rate**

Firms using AI reminder sequences see no-show rates drop from 30-40% to 10-15%. That's recovering 15-20% of consultations that would have been lost.

If you do 40 consultations per month and reduce no-shows from 40% to 15%, you've recovered 10 consultations. At a 50% conversion rate on consultations, that's 5 extra clients. Per month.

**What to Include in Reminders**

The most effective reminders include:
- Date, time, and location (or video call link)
- Attorney name and brief bio
- What to bring (documents, IDs, records)
- What to expect ("This is a free 30-minute consultation...")
- Easy rescheduling option (one-tap reply)

AI handles all of this automatically for every single appointment. No paralegal needs to remember. No sticky note system. No "did anyone remind Mrs. Johnson about tomorrow?"

Stop accepting 30% no-shows as normal. It's not normal. It's a fixable problem.
