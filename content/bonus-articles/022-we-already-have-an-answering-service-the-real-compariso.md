# 22. "We Already Have an Answering Service" — The Real Comparison

"We already have an answering service. We're covered."

Are you, though? Let's compare what you're getting to what you could be getting.

**Your Answering Service:**
- Answers the phone (sometimes after 4-5 rings)
- Takes a message: name, number, "they said something about a car accident"
- Sends you a text or email with the message
- Does NOT qualify the lead
- Does NOT answer questions about your firm
- Does NOT book appointments
- Does NOT follow up
- Does NOT integrate with your CRM
- Does NOT send confirmation texts or emails
- Costs $200-$500/month
- Has high turnover (your callers talk to a different person every time)

**AI Intake:**
- Answers in under 5 seconds (first ring)
- Has a full conversation with the caller
- Qualifies based on YOUR specific criteria
- Answers questions about your practice areas, fee structure, and attorneys
- Books consultations directly on your calendar
- Sends confirmation texts and emails
- Follows up 5, 10, 15 times via call, text, and email
- Integrates with your CRM (GHL, Salesforce, Clio, whatever)
- Maintains full context across all interactions
- Never has a bad day
- Never quits

**The Message Problem**

Here's the fundamental issue with answering services: a message is not a conversion. Someone calls your firm at 9 PM. They're anxious, they need help, they're ready to hire a lawyer. They get a pleasant voice that says "I'll have someone from the firm call you back tomorrow."

Tomorrow. When the anxiety has faded. When they've called two other firms who actually answered their questions. When they've talked themselves out of it.

A message is a lead in limbo. An AI conversation is a lead in motion.

**The Qualification Gap**

Your answering service takes messages for everyone. The tire-kickers, the wrong practice area, the people who just want free advice, the people who qualify for your best cases. They all get the same treatment: a message.

AI qualifies in real-time. A personal injury lead with clear liability and significant injuries gets escalated immediately. A "I want to sue my neighbor for their dog barking" call gets handled politely but doesn't eat up attorney time.

**The Follow-Up Gap**

Answering services don't follow up. They take ONE message. That's it. If you don't call back, or you call back and the person doesn't answer, that's a dead lead.

AI follows up automatically. Day 1, day 3, day 7, day 14. Different channels — call, text, email. It keeps the lead warm until they convert or explicitly opt out.

**The Math**

Answering service: $300/month, takes messages
AI intake: Handles the entire front end of your practice

If AI converts even 3 extra cases per month, you've paid for a year of the service in a single month.

Your answering service is a band-aid. AI is the cure.
