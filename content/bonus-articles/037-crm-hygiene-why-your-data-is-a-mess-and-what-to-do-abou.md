# 37. CRM Hygiene: Why Your Data Is a Mess and What to Do About It

"Our CRM is a mess."

I hear this from every law firm I talk to. Every single one. And they're right — it IS a mess. But not for the reason they think.

**How CRMs Get Dirty**

1. **Inconsistent data entry.** One person enters "Personal Injury" another enters "PI" another enters "Auto Accident" and another enters "car wreck." Good luck running a report.

2. **Missing fields.** Name and phone number entered, everything else blank. No practice area, no lead source, no notes, no follow-up date.

3. **Duplicate contacts.** John Smith is in there three times — once from a web form, once from a phone call, once from an email. Three records, zero coordination.

4. **No status updates.** Leads are marked as "new" for six months. Did they convert? Did they say no? Did they hire someone else? Nobody updated the record.

5. **Staff turnover.** Every new hire has a different data entry style. Old hire's records are abandoned.

**Why CRM Hygiene Matters**

A dirty CRM means:
- You can't run accurate reports
- You can't segment for marketing
- You can't reactivate dead leads (because you don't know who's dead)
- You double-contact people (annoying)
- You miss follow-ups (expensive)
- You look unprofessional when a client calls and you can't find their record

**How AI Enforces CRM Hygiene**

When AI handles intake, every record gets entered identically. Same fields, same format, same data quality. Every. Single. Time.

- Full name: First Last (not "john smith" or "JOHN SMITH" or "Smith, John")
- Phone number: standardized format
- Practice area: selected from your predefined categories
- Lead source: captured automatically
- Status: updated automatically based on conversation outcome
- Notes: full conversation transcript attached
- Follow-up: automatically scheduled

No variation. No missing fields. No duplicates (AI checks for existing records before creating new ones).

**The Downstream Impact**

Clean CRM data means:
- Accurate reporting on lead sources and conversion rates
- Effective database reactivation campaigns
- Proper follow-up sequences that don't skip people
- Professional client experience when they call back
- Actual visibility into your pipeline

Your CRM is only as good as the data in it. AI makes sure the data is perfect from day one.
