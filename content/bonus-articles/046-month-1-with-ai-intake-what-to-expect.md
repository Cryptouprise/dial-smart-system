# 46. Month 1 with AI Intake: What to Expect

You signed up. The system is live. What happens in the first 30 days? Here's the honest truth — no hype, no fairy tales.

**Week 1: The Learning Curve**

The first week is calibration. The AI is live and responding to leads, but you're still getting used to it. Common experiences:

- "Wait, the AI already called that person?" — Yes. In 5 seconds. You'll stop being surprised by week 2.
- "The AI booked an appointment without telling me." — It told you. Check your calendar notifications. This is the new normal.
- "Some of the qualification questions aren't quite right." — Expected. We fine-tune based on your feedback. Tell us what to adjust.
- "My receptionist seems confused about her role." — She should be. Her role just changed from "answer everything" to "support in-office clients." That's an upgrade.

**Week 2: The First Results**

By week 2, you'll notice:

- Leads that would have gone to voicemail are now qualified and scheduled
- Your calendar is fuller than it's been in months
- You're getting after-hours consultations from leads that previously vanished
- Your CRM has cleaner, more complete data than ever before
- Follow-up sequences are running without anyone touching them

You'll also notice some kinks. Maybe the AI qualifies a lead that's clearly not a fit. Maybe the tone needs adjustment. Maybe the appointment booking flow needs tweaking. All normal. All fixable.

**Week 3: The Pattern Emerges**

By week 3, a new operational rhythm sets in:

- Attorney wakes up to pre-qualified consultations on their calendar
- Staff focuses on case work instead of phone tag
- No-show rates start dropping (reminders are working)
- You're seeing leads from after-hours and weekends that you never would have captured before

**Week 4: The First Numbers**

End of month 1. Time for data:

- How many leads were contacted in under 5 seconds?
- What's the new conversion rate vs. the previous 3-month average?
- How many after-hours leads were captured?
- What's the no-show rate with AI reminders vs. without?
- How many follow-up sequences are active?

Typical month 1 results:
- 15-25% improvement in conversion rate
- 50-70% reduction in response time
- 20-30% more consultations booked (mostly from after-hours capture)
- No-show rate down 10-15 percentage points

Is it perfect after month 1? No. Is it better than what you had? Absolutely. And it gets better every month from here.
