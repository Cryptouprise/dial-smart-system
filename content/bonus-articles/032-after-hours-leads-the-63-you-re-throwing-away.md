# 32. After-Hours Leads: The 63% You're Throwing Away

Here's a number that should concern you: 63% of legal inquiries happen outside traditional business hours.

Between 6 PM and 8 AM. On weekends. On holidays. When your office is dark and your phones are forwarded to an answering service that takes messages nobody reads until Monday.

63% of your leads.

**When People Need Lawyers**

People don't have legal emergencies on a schedule. Car accidents happen at 11 PM. DUI arrests happen at 2 AM. Domestic disputes happen on Sunday mornings. The moment someone realizes they need an attorney is almost never during business hours.

Even for non-emergency matters: people research attorneys at night. They Google "estate planning attorney" after the kids go to bed. They fill out a "free case evaluation" form during their lunch break. They text their friend for a lawyer recommendation at 8 PM.

These are ready buyers. And they're hitting your voicemail.

**The Answering Service Band-Aid**

"But we have an answering service for after hours."

Cool. What does your answering service actually do?

They answer. They take a message. They send you an email. That's it.

They don't:
- Qualify the lead
- Answer questions about your practice
- Book appointments
- Send follow-up texts
- Provide reassurance or guidance
- Warm-transfer to an attorney for urgent matters

A message isn't an intake. It's a sticky note with a phone number.

**What After-Hours AI Looks Like**

11:47 PM. Someone fills out your personal injury contact form.

AI calls them at 11:47 PM. "Hi, this is [AI] calling from [Your Firm]. I see you're reaching out about an injury case. I'd like to get some information and see how we can help. Is this a good time?"

Full intake conversation. Qualification. Questions answered. Consultation booked for tomorrow morning at 9:30 AM. Confirmation text sent. Reminder scheduled for 8:30 AM.

When your attorney walks in at 9 AM, they have a fully qualified consultation on their calendar with complete case details.

That's the after-hours experience your competitors aren't providing. Yet.

**The Revenue Opportunity**

63% of leads. If you get 100 leads per month, 63 of them came in after hours. If your voicemail kills even half of those, you're losing 30+ leads per month.

At $5,000 per client and a 25% conversion rate, that's 7-8 clients lost. $35,000-$40,000 per month.

Because you close at 5.
