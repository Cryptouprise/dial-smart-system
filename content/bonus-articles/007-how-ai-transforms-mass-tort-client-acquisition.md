# 7. How AI Transforms Mass Tort Client Acquisition

Mass tort is a volume game, and most firms are playing it with a peashooter.

You're running ads for Camp Lejeune, Roundup, AFFF, talcum powder, NEC baby formula — whatever the current mass tort is. You're spending serious money. $50,000, $100,000, sometimes $500,000 per month in advertising. And every lead that comes in needs to be qualified fast, because your cost per acquisition is already through the roof.

So what happens? A lead fills out your form at 8:47 PM. Your intake team went home at 6. The lead doesn't get called until tomorrow morning — if they're lucky. By then they've filled out 4 other forms (because mass tort leads always fill out multiple forms). The first firm to qualify and sign them wins.

Speed isn't just important in mass tort. It's everything.

**The Scale Problem**

Mass tort campaigns can generate hundreds or thousands of leads per day during peak advertising. No human intake team scales with that. You either hire an army of intake specialists (expensive and inconsistent) or you use an answering service (slow and terrible at qualification).

AI handles 10 leads or 10,000 leads with the same quality. Every single one gets called in under 5 seconds, qualified against your specific criteria, and routed to signing or rejection immediately.

**The Qualification Complexity**

Each mass tort has specific qualification criteria:

- Camp Lejeune: Stationed or lived at Camp Lejeune between 1953-1987? Duration? Specific health conditions?
- Roundup: Used Roundup for how long? Diagnosed with Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma?
- AFFF: Exposure type? Proximity to military base or airport? Cancer diagnosis?

AI handles branching qualification logic that would take a human 15-20 minutes in under 5 minutes. It knows exactly which questions to ask, in what order, and what answers qualify or disqualify.

**The Multi-Touch Problem**

Mass tort leads need nurturing. Many people saw an ad, filled out a form on impulse, and then forgot about it. Or they're unsure if they qualify. Or they need to find medical records before they can answer your questions.

AI follows up relentlessly: "Hi James, we're still working on your potential Camp Lejeune case. We just need your diagnosis date and the name of your treating physician to move forward. Can you get that information this week?"

That text on day 5, day 10, day 15 — it converts leads that every other firm gave up on.

**The Sign-Up Rate**

Mass tort is all about cost per signed case. If your cost per lead is $200 and your sign-up rate is 5%, your cost per signed case is $4,000. If AI bumps your sign-up rate to 8% through faster response and better follow-up, your cost per signed case drops to $2,500. On a campaign spending $100K/month, that's the difference between 25 and 40 signed cases.

Forty cases instead of twenty-five. Same ad spend. That's the power of not letting leads rot in your CRM overnight.

Mass tort is an arms race. AI is the weapon you're not using yet.
