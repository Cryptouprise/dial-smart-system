# 24. "We Don't Do Outbound" — Here's Why You Should

"We're a law firm. We don't do outbound calls."

I get it. It feels salesy. It feels beneath the dignity of the legal profession. Attorneys went to law school, not sales school.

But let me reframe this: outbound isn't cold calling strangers. It's following up with people who already reached out to YOU and didn't get served properly.

**The Database You're Sitting On**

Right now, in your CRM, there are hundreds — maybe thousands — of leads that:

- Filled out your form but never booked a consultation
- Had a consultation but never signed
- Called once, didn't answer your callback, and fell into the void
- Were interested six months ago but the timing wasn't right
- Were referred by someone but never followed through

Those people already raised their hand. They already said "I might need a lawyer." You already paid to acquire them. And they're just... sitting there. Rotting.

That's not a cold database. That's golden garbage. The most interested people, smallest volume, highest conversion potential.

**What AI Outbound Looks Like**

This isn't a boiler room. This is targeted, personalized re-engagement:

"Hi Sarah, this is [AI] calling from [Your Firm]. You reached out to us back in January about a family law matter. I wanted to check in and see if you still needed assistance. Is this a good time?"

That's it. No hard sell. No pressure. Just a check-in from the firm she already contacted.

**The Results**

When firms activate outbound re-engagement on their existing database, they typically see:

- 15-25% of "dead" leads re-engage
- 5-10% convert to new clients
- $0 additional marketing spend required

You already paid for these leads. AI is just collecting on the investment.

**The Ethical Framework**

Worried about bar rules? Outbound contact with people who previously initiated contact with your firm is not solicitation. It's follow-up. There's a massive legal distinction. They contacted you first. You're calling back. That's called good client service.

**The Revenue You're Leaving Behind**

If you have 500 unconverted leads in your database and AI re-engages 20% (100 leads) and converts 10% of those (10 clients) at $5,000 per case, that's $50,000 in revenue from leads you'd already written off.

That's not outbound. That's found money.
