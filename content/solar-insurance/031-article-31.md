## P&C Agencies: How AI Handles the Renewal Cycle Automatically

### Renewals: Your Biggest Revenue and Biggest Headache

For P&C (Property & Casualty) agencies, renewals are the lifeblood. They're also the most predictable and the most neglected part of the business.

Think about it: you know EXACTLY when every client's policy renews. It's literally in your system, 12 months out, with the exact date. This is the most predictable revenue event in any business.

And yet, here's what happens at most agencies:

**90 days before renewal:** Nothing.
**60 days before renewal:** Maybe the AMS sends an automated email that goes to spam.
**30 days before renewal:** Agent might notice the renewal coming up. "Oh, I should call the Smiths."
**15 days before renewal:** Carrier sends the renewal notice. If the rate went up, the client gets mad and starts shopping.
**7 days before renewal:** Client calls: "Why did my rate go up 25%? I'm switching."
**Renewal day:** Client either auto-renews (dodged a bullet) or has already signed with another agency (you lost).

This is reactive renewal management. You're waiting for the client to notice their renewal and hoping they don't leave. That's not a strategy. That's prayer.

### The AI Proactive Renewal System

Here's what proactive renewal management looks like with AI:

**120 days before renewal:**
AI flags upcoming renewals and begins pre-renewal analysis:
- Pull current premium and coverage
- Check for claims in the current term
- Note any life changes (added vehicle, home renovation, new driver)

**90 days before renewal:**
AI reaches out to the client:
"Hi [Name]! Your [auto/home] policy renews on [date]. We're going to start shopping rates for you now to make sure you're getting the best deal. A couple quick questions: any changes since last year? New vehicles, home improvements, changes in driving?"

This accomplishes several things:
1. Client knows you're proactively working for them (loyalty)
2. You capture life changes that affect coverage/pricing
3. You start the conversation on YOUR terms, not the carrier's
4. Client feels valued (you reached out 90 days early!)

**60 days before renewal:**
AI follows up with update:
"Good news — we've reviewed your coverage and here's what we're seeing. [Agent] will give you a call this week to go over your options."

Agent calls with a re-marketing analysis. If there's a better rate, they present it. If the current carrier is still best, they explain why — and the client feels great about staying.

**30 days before renewal:**
AI confirms:
"Your [policy type] renews on [date]. Everything looks good. Any questions before we finalize?"

**14 days before renewal:**
Text: "Just confirming — your policy renews on [date]. You're all set with [carrier]. Payment will process as usual."

**Post-renewal:**
Text: "Your policy has renewed! Here's a quick summary of your coverage. Anything change in the next year, just text us anytime."

### Cross-Sell During Renewal

Renewal is the perfect time to cross-sell, and AI makes it systematic:

**Auto-only clients:**
"While we're reviewing your auto policy, I noticed you don't have a homeowner's policy with us. Bundling could save you [X]%. Want me to run a quick quote?"

**Home-only clients:**
"Have you considered bundling your auto insurance? We often save clients [X]% when we combine home and auto."

**No umbrella:**
"With your assets, an umbrella policy might make sense. It's usually only [$X/month] for an extra million in protection."

**No life insurance:**
"Quick thought — do you have life insurance? With your mortgage, it's worth making sure your family is covered."

AI identifies the cross-sell opportunity and initiates the conversation. If the client is interested, it connects them with the agent. If not, no harm done.

Typical cross-sell rate when proactively offered during renewal: 12-18%. On 2,000 clients, that's 240-360 additional policies per year.

### Automating the Renewal Workflow

Here's the full AI renewal workflow:

1. **120 days out:** Flag and pre-analyze
2. **90 days out:** Client outreach + life change questionnaire
3. **75 days out:** Agent notification to begin re-marketing
4. **60 days out:** Client update with options
5. **45 days out:** Cross-sell opportunity presentation
6. **30 days out:** Renewal confirmation
7. **14 days out:** Final confirmation
8. **0 days:** Renewal processed
9. **+7 days:** Post-renewal check-in
10. **+30 days:** Review/referral request

Every step automated. Every client touched. Every renewal proactively managed.
