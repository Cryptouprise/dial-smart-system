### Every Touch. Automated.

Let me walk you through the lifecycle of a client — from stranger to superfan — entirely managed by one platform:

**Stage 1: Lead Capture**
Prospect fills out a form. Within 5 seconds: call, text, email. AI qualifies, answers questions, and books an appointment.

**Stage 2: Pre-Appointment**
AI sends preparation materials. Gathers necessary documents. Confirms the appointment multiple times. Recovers no-shows automatically.

**Stage 3: The Sale**
Agent runs the appointment with a fully prepared, pre-qualified prospect. Closes the deal.

**Stage 4: Onboarding**
AI sends welcome messages: "Your policy is active! Here's what you need to know..." ID cards, payment info, contact details, what to do in an emergency.

**Stage 5: Ongoing Service**
AI handles routine questions (via text or call). Escalates complex issues to the agent. Logs everything.

**Stage 6: Claims Support**
When a client has a claim, AI initiates the follow-up sequence: check-ins at Day 2, 5, 7, 14, 30. Agent is notified of any issues.

**Stage 7: Renewal Management**
90 days before renewal, AI begins the proactive renewal process. Re-marketing. Client communication. Rate comparison messaging.

**Stage 8: Cross-Sell**
AI identifies coverage gaps and reaches out at the right time. Bundles home with auto. Adds umbrella. Introduces life insurance.

**Stage 9: Referral Generation**
After positive interactions (especially post-claim or post-renewal), AI requests referrals: "We love working with you! Know anyone else who could use a great insurance agent?"

**Stage 10: Win-Back**
If a client does leave, AI enters a win-back sequence: "We miss you at [Agency]! Rates have changed — want to see if we can earn your business back?"

Ten stages. Every one automated. Every touch handled. From prospect to client to advocate — the entire lifecycle managed by one platform.
