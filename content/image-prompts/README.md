## GENERATION NOTES

**Color Palette:**
- Primary background: #0A0A0F (near-black), #0F1629 (deep navy)
- Primary accent: #3B82F6 (electric blue)
- Secondary accent: #F59E0B (gold/amber)
- Positive indicators: #10B981 (emerald green)
- Alert/negative: #EF4444 (red)
- Neutral: #6B7280 (gray)

**Platform Recommendations:**
- fal.ai with FLUX models for photorealistic and product shots
- Gemini Imagen 3 for abstract/conceptual compositions
- Add "professional marketing photography" or "premium SaaS product visualization" to prompts for better results
- Append "no text, no watermarks, no logos" if the generator tends to add unwanted typography

**Post-Processing:**
- All images will need text overlay in Canva or Figma after generation
- Blog heroes: leave clear space (left third or top third) for headlines
- Social squares: leave top and bottom bands for text
- Brand images: some are templates, some are standalone visuals
