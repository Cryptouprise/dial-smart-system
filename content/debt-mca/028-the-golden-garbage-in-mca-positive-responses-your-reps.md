# Article 28: The Golden Garbage in MCA: Positive Responses Your Reps Never Call Back

## The MCA Floor's Dirty Little Secret

Pull up your CRM right now. Filter for leads with any of these dispositions:
- "Call back"
- "Interested"
- "Send info"
- "Warm"
- "App sent - not completed"
- "Follow up needed"

How many leads are in there? 2,000? 10,000? 30,000?

Now filter by last activity date. How many of those leads haven't been touched in more than 7 days?

That number -- the untouched warm leads -- is what we call the Golden Garbage. And it's the most expensive waste in your operation.

These aren't cold leads. These aren't "not interested." These are merchants who TOLD YOUR REPS they were interested in funding and then got abandoned because there were always more fresh leads to dial.

In any other business, this would be insanity. A car dealership where customers walk in and say "I want to buy a car" and nobody helps them because the salespeople are busy waving at traffic.

## The Scale of the Problem

A typical MCA sales floor with 30 reps generates approximately 150-200 warm leads per day that don't immediately fund. Per day.

Over a month, that's 3,300-4,400 warm leads.

Reps follow up with maybe 20% of them. The other 80% -- roughly 2,640-3,520 warm leads per month -- go untouched. They sit in the CRM until someone runs a "clean up your pipeline" meeting, at which point reps mass-disposition them as "dead" to clean their dashboards.

They weren't dead. They were interested. They just needed one more call.

## AI: The Golden Garbage Collector

AI treats every positive response like the gold it is. No warm lead goes more than 24 hours without follow-up. No "send info" goes without a same-day check-in. No "app sent" goes without an application completion nudge within 4 hours.

Here's what the AI Golden Garbage recovery looks like:

**Day 0:** Positive disposition logged. AI immediately sends a personalized follow-up based on the specific interaction. Not a generic "following up" text. A contextual one:

For "call back": "Hey [Name], [Rep] mentioned you'd like a callback about business funding. I'm available right now or we can schedule a specific time. What works?"

For "send info": "Here's the overview [Rep] mentioned. Quick version: we fund $10K-$500K based on your monthly revenue. Takes about 48 hours. Reply with your monthly revenue and I'll tell you what you'd likely qualify for."

For "app started not completed": "Looks like you started the application -- great! I noticed it's not complete yet. The whole thing takes about 8 minutes. Want me to walk you through it?"

**Days 1-7:** Daily follow-ups. Mix of calls and texts. Each one referencing the original conversation and providing value.

**Days 7-30:** Bi-weekly touchpoints. Staying top of mind without being annoying.

## The Conversion Rate on Golden Garbage

Fresh cold leads in MCA: 1.5-3% funded rate.

Golden Garbage with AI follow-up: 8-15% funded rate.

Read that again. Golden Garbage converts at 3-5x the rate of fresh leads. Because these people already said yes. They just needed someone to follow through.

On those 3,000 monthly warm leads your reps abandon:
- Cold conversion (without follow-up): 30-90 funded deals IF someone ever called them
- AI follow-up conversion: 240-450 funded deals

The delta between "nobody followed up" and "AI followed up" is potentially hundreds of funded deals per month. At your average deal revenue, that's... well, you can do the math. And you should, because it's going to make you uncomfortable.
