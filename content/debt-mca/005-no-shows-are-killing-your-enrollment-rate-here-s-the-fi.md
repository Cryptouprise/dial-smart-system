# Article 5: No-Shows Are Killing Your Enrollment Rate. Here's the Fix.

## You Book 100 Appointments. 40 Don't Show Up. You're Fine With This?

No-shows in debt consolidation aren't a minor annoyance. They're a profit-killing epidemic that most companies treat as "just the way it is."

Here's what a typical debt company's appointment funnel looks like:

- 1,000 leads → 100 appointments booked → 60 show up → 30 enroll

That means 40% of your appointments -- people who said "yes, I want help, put me on the calendar" -- just vanish. Don't show. Don't call. Don't text. They're gone.

At a $40 lead cost and a 10% booking rate, each of those appointments cost you $400 to create. Forty no-shows? That's $16,000 in wasted pipeline. Per month.

And what does your team do about it? Maybe a confirmation call the day before. Maybe a text morning-of. Maybe nothing at all because they're too busy dialing new leads.

## Why Debt Leads No-Show (It's Not What You Think)

Most companies think no-shows are flaky people who weren't serious. Sometimes that's true. But more often, the reasons are:

**Fear.** They booked the appointment in a moment of panic. Then they calmed down and the embarrassment took over. Talking to a stranger about $50,000 in debt? That's terrifying.

**Life happened.** They had to work. Kids got sick. Car broke down. Something came up and they forgot because your appointment wasn't their top priority that day.

**They got cold.** Three days between booking and the appointment is an eternity. They started second-guessing. They Googled reviews. They got scared.

**Nobody reminded them.** Your single confirmation text wasn't enough to cut through the noise of their life.

**They enrolled somewhere else.** Between booking with you and the appointment, another company called them, caught them at the right time, and closed them.

Notice something? Almost none of these reasons mean the person doesn't need debt help. They all mean the person needed MORE engagement between booking and the appointment, not less.

## The AI No-Show Prevention System

Here's what AI does between the moment an appointment is booked and the moment it's supposed to happen:

**Immediately after booking:**
- Confirmation text with date, time, what to expect, and what documents to have ready
- Confirmation email with the same info plus a calendar invite
- "Welcome to [Company]" message that sets expectations and reduces anxiety

**24 hours before:**
- Reminder text: "Looking forward to speaking with you tomorrow at [time]. Here's a quick overview of what we'll cover so you feel prepared."
- This isn't just a reminder -- it's pre-framing the call to reduce fear

**2 hours before:**
- Text: "Just a reminder we're chatting in 2 hours. If you need to reschedule, reply here and we'll find a new time. No pressure."
- Offering a reschedule catches the people who would otherwise ghost

**15 minutes before:**
- Text: "We're calling in 15 minutes! If you need a few extra minutes, just let me know."

**At appointment time, if no answer:**
- AI calls immediately
- If no answer: text within 30 seconds -- "Tried calling just now -- looks like you might be tied up. Want me to try again in 15 minutes or would another time work better?"
- Follow-up call 15 minutes later
- Follow-up call 1 hour later
- Reschedule offer that evening

**Post no-show recovery (Days 1-7):**
- Daily text and call attempts for the first 3 days
- Reschedule messaging for the full week
- Re-engagement campaign for those who don't respond
