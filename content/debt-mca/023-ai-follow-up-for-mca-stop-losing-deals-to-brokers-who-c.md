# Article 23: AI Follow-Up for MCA: Stop Losing Deals to Brokers Who Call Faster

## The MCA Follow-Up Black Hole

In MCA, the deal isn't done when the merchant says "I'm interested." It's done when the money hits their bank account. And between "interested" and "funded," there's a massive gap where deals go to die.

Here's the typical MCA follow-up timeline:

**Day 0:** Merchant expresses interest. Rep sends application.
**Day 1:** Rep checks if application was completed. It wasn't.
**Day 2:** Rep calls to follow up on application. Gets voicemail.
**Day 3:** Rep has 40 new leads. Merchant application follow-up moves to bottom of priority list.
**Day 5:** Merchant finally starts the application. Gets confused on a section. Stops.
**Day 7:** Rep remembers to follow up. Merchant says "oh yeah, I got busy." Promises to finish.
**Day 10:** Application still not complete. Rep has mentally moved on.
**Day 14:** Merchant gets called by another broker who follows up faster. Merchant completes THEIR application instead.

Deal lost. Not because your offer was worse. Because your follow-up was worse.

## The Application Completion Problem

In MCA, getting a merchant to SAY yes is the easy part. Getting them to COMPLETE the application, send bank statements, and respond to stipulations -- that's where deals die.

Application completion rates in MCA: 35-45% without aggressive follow-up. That means more than HALF of merchants who express interest never finish the paperwork.

Why? Because they're small business owners. They're busy. They're running a restaurant or a trucking company or a retail shop. Filling out a lending application is not the most important thing on their to-do list today, even if they need the money.

They need to be reminded. Nudged. Helped through the process. Multiple times.

And your reps won't do it because there are always more fresh leads to call.

## AI Follow-Up for MCA: The Complete System

**Application sent (Hour 0):** AI texts the merchant with the application link and a clear explanation of what's needed and how long it takes.

**Not started (Hour 4):** AI text: "Just checking -- did you get the application link? I can resend it or walk you through it over the phone if that's easier."

**Not started (Hour 24):** AI call: "Hey [Name], I wanted to make sure you got everything you need to complete the application. A lot of merchants knock it out in about 10 minutes. Is there anything holding you up?"

**Partially complete (detected):** AI text: "I see you started the application -- nice! Looks like you stopped on the bank statement section. You can just take a photo of your latest statement and upload it. Need help?"

**Bank statements needed (after application):** AI texts 3x in 48 hours with instructions on how to download/photograph/upload. Offers to walk through it on a call.

**Stipulations (after underwriting):** AI immediately notifies the merchant of any additional documents needed. Follows up every 12 hours until received.

**Final approval:** AI notifies merchant of approval and confirms funding details.
