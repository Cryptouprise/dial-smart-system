## The Dirty Secret of Every MCA Sales Floor

Your ISO reps are sitting on a goldmine and they don't even know it. Or worse -- they know it and they're too busy dialing to care.

Here's what happens on a typical MCA sales floor every single day:

A rep dials 200 numbers. Gets 30 conversations. Qualifies 10 merchants. Submits 5 to funders. Gets 2 funded.

The rep celebrates the 2 funded deals. The manager high-fives them. Commission hits the account.

Nobody asks the important question: what happened to the other 198 dials?

Let me tell you. Out of those 198:
- 170 went to voicemail or didn't pick up
- 15 said "I'm busy, call back"
- 5 said "send me information"
- 3 started a conversation but the rep rushed them and they lost interest
- 3 said "not right now but maybe in a few months"
- 2 had positive conversations but the rep forgot to follow up

That's 28 leads with some level of interest that your rep will never touch again. Multiply by 50 reps. Multiply by 250 working days. That's 350,000 warm-ish contacts per year that nobody follows up on.

At even a 1% conversion rate on follow-up, that's 3,500 additional funded deals your team left on the table. What's your average commission per deal? $2,000? $5,000?

Do the math. Then take a deep breath.

## Why ISO Reps Can't Help It

Your reps aren't bad at their jobs. They're operating in a system that rewards activity over results.

The MCA sales floor culture is simple: dial more, make more. Managers track dials. Leaderboards show talk time. The energy on the floor is about VOLUME.

So when a rep has a warm lead who said "call me back Thursday," they're not going to set a reminder and carefully follow up on Thursday. They're going to dial 200 more numbers tomorrow and hope that Thursday lead calls THEM back.

Spoiler: they won't.

One in the hand is better than two in the bush -- and MCA reps always chase the bush. Fresh dials. New prospects. The adrenaline of the cold call. It's addictive. And it leaves a trail of abandoned warm leads in its wake.

## What AI Does With Your Reps' Leftovers

AI picks up where your reps give up. Every lead that gets a positive disposition -- call back, interested, send info, busy but warm -- gets immediately entered into an AI follow-up sequence.

**"Call me back Thursday"** -- AI calls back Thursday. And if they don't pick up, AI calls Friday. And texts Saturday. And calls Monday. And follows up for 30 days until they either talk or explicitly say no.

**"Send me information"** -- AI sends info immediately. Then follows up 24 hours later: "Did you get a chance to look at the information I sent? I can run some quick numbers if you tell me your monthly revenue." Then follows up 3 more times.

**"Not right now"** -- AI adds them to a 6-month nurture sequence. Checks in monthly. When cash flow gets tight (and it will), your company is the first call they think of.

**"Forgot to follow up"** -- doesn't happen anymore. AI doesn't forget. Ever.
