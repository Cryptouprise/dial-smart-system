## In MCA, Speed IS the Product

When a business owner needs capital, they don't care about your funder relationships. They don't care about your years in the industry. They don't care about your office on Wall Street.

They care about one thing: how fast can you get me money?

Fair? Maybe not. True? Absolutely.

## The MCA Speed Problem

MCA leads are unique. Unlike debt or insurance, where the consumer might take weeks to decide, MCA merchants often need to make a decision within days. Payroll is Friday. The supplier needs payment by end of week. The equipment needs to be fixed today.

When they fill out a form, they want a call within minutes. Not an email. Not a "we'll get back to you within 24 hours." A phone call from a human (or human-sounding AI) who says "I can help. Let's talk numbers."

The average MCA broker responds in 2-5 hours. The best respond in 30-60 minutes. Almost nobody responds in under 5 minutes.
