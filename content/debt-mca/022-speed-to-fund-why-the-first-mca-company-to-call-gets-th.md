## Merchants Don't Shop for MCA. They Grab the First Lifeline.

Let me explain something about MCA leads that changes everything about how you should think about speed.

When a merchant applies for a cash advance, they're not comparison shopping like someone buying a TV. They need money. Now. Payroll is Friday. A supplier invoice is due. Equipment broke. Tax bill came.

They filled out one form. Maybe two. And they're sitting there with their phone in their hand, anxious, hoping someone calls and says "yes, we can help."

The first company that calls with a credible "yes" gets the deal. Not the company with the best rates. Not the company with the highest advance amount. The first one that shows up with a solution.

Why? Because the merchant's problem is urgency, not optimization. They're not going to wait 3 days to compare 5 offers. They need funding THIS WEEK. The company that demonstrates speed in calling is the company the merchant trusts to demonstrate speed in funding.
