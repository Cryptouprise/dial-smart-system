## Where the 9,800 Actually Went

Let's break down what happened to those 9,800 unfunded leads:

**5,000 - Never reached.** Voicemail, wrong number, disconnected, business closed. These are genuinely low-value. But "never reached" doesn't mean "reached and not interested." There's a difference.

**2,000 - Reached but not ready.** "Not right now." "Call me back later." "I just got funded." These are TIMING issues, not interest issues. In 3-6 months, many of these merchants will need funding.

**1,200 - Interested but didn't convert.** Had conversations. Expressed interest. But the process stalled -- didn't complete application, didn't send statements, got a better offer, got scared of daily payments.

**800 - Partially qualified.** Revenue was borderline. Time in business was short. Industry was risky. They didn't qualify for your A-paper funders but might qualify for B-paper.

**500 - Positive interactions that fell through cracks.** Said "send me info," expressed interest in a text, had a good conversation that the rep didn't follow up on. The Golden Garbage.

**300 - Didn't qualify but will.** New businesses that are 6 months from having enough revenue history. Growing businesses that will cross your minimum thresholds soon.
