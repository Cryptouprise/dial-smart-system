# Article 31: The MCA Broker's Secret Weapon: AI That Submits to 10 Funders Simultaneously

## The Broker Bottleneck

If you're an MCA broker, your value proposition is simple: get the merchant the best offer from the widest range of funders. More submissions = more approvals = better terms for the merchant = more commission for you.

In theory.

In practice, your reps submit to 2-3 funders per deal. Maybe 4 if they're ambitious. Why? Because each submission takes time:
- Different funder portals
- Different application formats
- Different documentation requirements
- Different follow-up protocols

A rep who's managing 15 active deals at various stages can't also submit each one to 10 different funders and manage the approval process across all of them. The administrative overhead is crushing.

So they take shortcuts. Submit to their 2-3 go-to funders. Accept the first approval. Move on.

The merchant might have gotten 3 points better from funder #7. But your rep never submitted there because they ran out of time.

## AI Submission at Scale

AI transforms the submission process:

**Universal application.** AI collects all information once in a standardized format. Every data point needed by every funder on your panel.

**Automated formatting.** AI reformats the application data for each funder's specific requirements. Funder A wants revenue monthly, Funder B wants it weekly, Funder C wants a specific calculation -- AI handles all of it.

**Simultaneous submission.** One merchant application goes to 8, 10, or 15 funders at once. Not serially. Simultaneously.

**Approval tracking.** AI monitors each submission. Follows up on missing stipulations. Tracks approval status. Compiles offers as they come in.

**Offer comparison.** When multiple offers arrive, AI presents them side-by-side: amount, factor rate, term, payment frequency, total cost. Your closer reviews the comparison and recommends the best option to the merchant.

## What This Means for Your Brokerage

**More approvals per deal.** Submitting to 10 funders instead of 3 means more bites at the apple. Merchants who get declined by your go-to funders might get approved by funder #8.

**Better terms.** More offers = more competition = better terms for the merchant. When you can show a merchant 5 different offers, they trust you. They refer you. They come back for renewals.

**Faster funding.** AI doesn't wait for funder #1 to decline before submitting to funder #2. Everything goes out simultaneously. First approval wins.

**Higher close rates.** When you present a merchant with the best offer from 10 funders instead of the only offer from 2 funders, they say yes more often.

**Less rep time per deal.** Your reps spend time closing, not doing data entry across 10 funder portals. Each rep handles 3-4x more deal volume.

## The Competitive Moat

In a commoditized MCA broker market, your competitive advantage is speed and options. AI gives you both.

The broker who can say "I submitted you to 10 funders and got you 6 offers in 24 hours" beats the broker who says "I sent it to my guy and should hear back in 2-3 days."

Every time.

**More funders. More offers. More funded deals. Less work. That's AI for MCA brokers.**
