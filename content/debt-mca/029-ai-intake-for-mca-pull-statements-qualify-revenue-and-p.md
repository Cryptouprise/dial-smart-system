# Article 29: AI Intake for MCA: Pull Statements, Qualify Revenue, and Price the Deal -- Automatically

## From Lead to Offer in 15 Minutes. No Humans Required.

Here's the MCA intake process in most shops:

1. Rep talks to merchant (5-10 minutes)
2. Rep qualifies verbally (2-3 minutes)
3. Rep sends application link (30 seconds)
4. Merchant fills out application... eventually (1-5 days)
5. Rep follows up on missing bank statements... maybe (1-3 days)
6. Merchant uploads statements... eventually (1-3 days)
7. Rep submits to funder (30 minutes)
8. Funder underwrites (1-2 days)
9. Funded (1 day)

Total time from first contact to funded: 5-14 days. And that's if nothing goes wrong, nobody gets busy, and the merchant doesn't ghost during steps 4-6.

Now here's AI intake:

1. AI calls merchant within 5 seconds of lead arrival
2. AI qualifies: monthly revenue, time in business, industry, existing advances (3 minutes)
3. AI sends application link via text during the call (0 seconds)
4. AI walks merchant through application in real-time (8 minutes)
5. AI requests bank statements: "Can you take a photo of your last 3 months of statements and text them to this number?" (merchant does it while on the call or within hours)
6. AI follows up on missing statements every 4 hours until received
7. AI packages everything and submits to funder panel
8. Funder underwrites (1 day)
9. Funded (1 day)

Total time: 2-3 days. Half the time or less. And the merchant never waited more than 5 seconds for a response at any point.

## The Statement Collection Problem (Solved)

Bank statement collection is the #1 bottleneck in MCA funding. Merchants hate sending them. They're embarrassed about their balances. They don't know how to download from their bank portal. They're busy running a business.

Reps send the request, wait 3 days, follow up once, wait 2 more days, and then either chase harder or give up.

AI takes a completely different approach:

**During the initial call:** "This might sound silly, but the fastest way to get this done is to pull up your bank app right now and screenshot the last 3 months. Can you do that while we're talking? I'll walk you through it."

**If merchant can't do it right then:** AI sends a step-by-step text guide for downloading statements from their specific bank (AI can ask which bank they use and customize the instructions).

**Hour 4:** "Hey [Name], did you get a chance to grab those statements? The sooner we have them, the sooner I can get you an offer."

**Hour 12:** "Quick reminder -- we just need 3 months of bank statements to get you funded. Here's the upload link again: [link]"

**Hour 24:** "I know you're busy running your business. Want me to call you and walk through downloading the statements? Takes 5 minutes."

**Hour 36:** AI calls and walks through it live.

Statement collection rates go from 45% to 80%+ with AI persistence. That alone doubles the number of deals that make it to underwriting.

## Revenue Qualification and Pricing

AI doesn't just collect information -- it processes it. When statements come in, AI can extract:
- Monthly revenue (average and trend)
- Daily balance patterns
- Existing MCA ACH withdrawals (stacking detection)
- NSF frequency
- Revenue consistency

Based on this analysis, AI can determine:
- Which funders on your panel this merchant qualifies for
- Approximate advance amounts
- Estimated factor rates
- Daily/weekly payment amounts

This means when your closer gets on the phone with the merchant, they're not starting from scratch. They have a complete financial picture and preliminary pricing ready to present.

**AI doesn't just collect data. It processes it, qualifies it, and prices the deal before a human ever gets involved.**
