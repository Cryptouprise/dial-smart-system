### What Is Golden Garbage?

Golden garbage is every lead that showed interest but didn't convert. The person who called about a personal injury case but your intake coordinator was at lunch. The potential client who filled out your website form on a Saturday night and never got a callback. The guy who said "let me think about it" and nobody ever followed up.

These aren't cold leads. These are warm, pre-qualified, already-interested human beings who wanted to hire a lawyer and nobody bothered to close them.

And they're sitting in your system right now. Hundreds of them. Maybe thousands.

Your intake team gets 30 new leads a day. They're busy chasing the fresh ones because new feels exciting. Meanwhile, last Tuesday's leads — the ones who said "I'm interested but I'm at work right now, can you call me back tonight?" — those leads are dead to your team.

This happens every single day in every law firm in America. And nobody tracks it because nobody wants to look at the number.
