### What the GHL Buddy Does

The GHL Buddy is an AI layer that lives inside your GHL account and turns it into a fully autonomous client acquisition machine:

**Payment Automation:** Invoice generation, delivery, reminders, and payment plan management — all running through GHL's payment tools, managed by AI.
