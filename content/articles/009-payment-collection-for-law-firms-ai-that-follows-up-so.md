## Article 33: Payment Collection for Law Firms — AI That Follows Up So You Don't Have to

Let me tell you about the most uncomfortable part of running a law firm: asking clients for money.

Your attorneys went to law school to practice law, not to be bill collectors. Your staff doesn't want to make "your payment is overdue" calls. And your clients — the ones who owe you — are screening your calls because they know exactly why you're reaching out.

The result? Accounts receivable that would make an accountant cry.

### The Collection Problem in Legal

Law firms have notoriously bad collection rates. Industry averages show that firms write off 10-15% of billable work as uncollectable. For smaller firms and solo practitioners, that number can hit 20-30%.

That's not lost revenue from cases you didn't win. That's money you earned — hours you worked, value you delivered — that you're never going to see.

Why? Because your collection process looks like this:

1. Send an invoice (email, maybe)
2. Wait 30 days
3. Send a reminder email
4. Wait 30 more days
5. Have your office manager make an awkward phone call
6. Client says "I'll send it next week"
7. Wait two more weeks
8. Consider sending to collections (but the amount isn't worth the hassle)
9. Write it off

### How AI Changes the Collection Game

AI payment follow-up removes the human discomfort from the process while being more persistent and consistent than any human team could be:

**Automated invoice delivery** — AI sends invoices via text, email, and (if needed) phone call. Includes a one-click payment link in every communication.

**Graduated reminder sequence** — Starting friendly and escalating over time:
- Day 1: Invoice sent with payment link
- Day 3: Friendly text reminder
- Day 7: Phone call from AI — "Just making sure you received the invoice"
- Day 14: Follow-up text with payment plan options
- Day 21: More direct communication about outstanding balance
- Day 30+: Escalation protocol (your choice on severity)

**Payment plan negotiation** — AI can offer pre-approved payment plans based on your rules. "I understand the full amount is a lot right now. Would it help to break it into three monthly payments?" This one feature alone recovers a massive percentage of receivables.

**Multiple payment channels** — Text-to-pay links, online portal, phone payments. Remove every friction point between the client and their wallet.

### Why AI Is Better at This Than Your Staff

Three reasons:

**No emotions.** AI doesn't feel awkward asking for money. It doesn't soften the message to the point of uselessness. It doesn't skip the call because it doesn't want confrontation. It just follows the sequence, professionally, every time.

**No forgetting.** Every follow-up happens on schedule. No leads falling through the cracks. No "I meant to call them but got busy."

**No judgment from the client.** Counterintuitively, clients are often more comfortable discussing payment with an AI system than with a person from the firm. There's less perceived judgment, less embarrassment, and more willingness to discuss payment plans honestly.
