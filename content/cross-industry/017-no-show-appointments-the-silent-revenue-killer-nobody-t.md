## Why People No-Show

Understanding why people no-show is the first step to fixing it:

1. **They forgot.** The most common reason. Life is busy. They booked the appointment Tuesday and completely forgot by Thursday.

2. **Their urgency faded.** When they booked, they were excited. By appointment day, the urgency has cooled. Other priorities took over.

3. **They got cold feet.** They're interested but nervous about committing. Avoiding the appointment is easier than saying no.

4. **They found another option.** Between booking and appointment day, a competitor got to them.

5. **Something legitimately came up.** Work emergency, sick kid, car trouble. Life happens.

Notice that only #5 is truly unrecoverable. Reasons #1-4 are all fixable with the right system.

## The Reminder Framework That Cuts No-Shows in Half

Here's what our AI does for every booked appointment:

**Immediately after booking:** Confirmation text + email with appointment details, what to expect, and what to bring.

**24 hours before:** First reminder. "Hey [Name], just confirming your appointment tomorrow at [time]. Looking forward to it! Reply YES to confirm."

**2 hours before:** Second reminder. "Quick reminder — your appointment is in 2 hours at [time]. Any questions before we meet?"

**30 minutes before:** Final reminder. "See you in 30 minutes! Here's the address/link/details one more time."

This multi-touch reminder sequence cuts no-show rates by 40-60% across our platform. Why? Because it addresses the top 4 reasons people no-show:

- They can't forget (4 reminders)
- Their urgency is maintained (consistent engagement)
- Cold feet are warmed by friendly, reassuring communication
- They're less likely to shop competitors when actively engaged with you

## The No-Show Recovery Engine

Even with perfect reminders, some people will still no-show. What happens then?

In most companies: nothing. The rep waits 15 minutes, marks it as a no-show, and moves on. That lead is dead. Nobody follows up. Nobody reschedules. The money evaporates.

In our system, the moment a no-show is detected:

**Within 5 minutes:** AI sends a text: "Hey [Name], looks like we missed each other for our appointment. No worries — things come up! When would be a good time to reschedule?"

**Within 30 minutes:** AI calls. If they answer, it has a natural conversation: "Hi [Name], we had an appointment earlier — just wanted to make sure everything's okay and see if we can find a better time."

**Within 2 hours:** AI sends an email with easy rescheduling links.

**Next day:** If no response, another text: "Still interested in [product/service]? Happy to work around your schedule."
