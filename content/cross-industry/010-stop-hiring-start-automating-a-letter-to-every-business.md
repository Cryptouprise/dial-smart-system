## The True Cost of a Sales Rep

You think your sales rep costs $60,000 a year. Here's what that rep actually costs:

**Direct compensation:**
- Base salary: $45,000-65,000
- Commission/bonus: $15,000-40,000
- Benefits (health, dental, 401k): $8,000-15,000
- Payroll taxes: $5,000-8,000

**Subtotal: $73,000-128,000/year**

**Indirect costs (the hidden ones):**
- Recruiting/hiring: $3,000-8,000 (amortized, since average tenure is 14 months)
- Training/ramp-up: $5,000-10,000 (2-3 months to full productivity)
- Management overhead: $8,000-12,000 (your managers' time spent supervising, coaching, reviewing)
- Equipment/software: $2,000-4,000
- Office space: $3,000-8,000
- HR/admin: $2,000-3,000
- Turnover cost: $5,000-15,000 (amortized across expected tenure)

**Indirect subtotal: $28,000-60,000/year**

**True total cost per sales rep: $101,000-188,000/year**

And remember: that rep is only selling 30% of the time. The other 70%? You're paying $101K-188K for someone to dial numbers, leave voicemails, update CRM fields, and take lunch breaks.

Effective cost of their actual selling time: $337,000-627,000 per year per rep.

Read that again. You're paying a third to half a million dollars — in effective terms — for each rep's selling hours.

## The Turnover Tax

Let's talk about the elephant in the room: turnover.

Average sales rep tenure is 14 months. Fourteen months. You spend 2-3 months ramping them up. They're productive for 8-10 months. Then they leave.

Every single time a rep leaves, you lose:
- 2-3 months of ramp time for the replacement
- Institutional knowledge that walked out the door
- Client relationships that need to be rebuilt
- Team morale (other reps see the turnover and start updating their resumes)
- $15,000-25,000 in direct replacement costs

If you have 10 reps and average tenure is 14 months, you're replacing roughly 8-9 reps per year. That's $120,000-225,000 per year in pure turnover costs. Money that produces absolutely nothing.

AI doesn't quit. AI doesn't get recruited by a competitor. AI doesn't need to be replaced. Ever.
