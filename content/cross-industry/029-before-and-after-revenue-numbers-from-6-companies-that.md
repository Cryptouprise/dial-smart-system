## The Pattern

Six companies. Six different industries. Six different sizes. Same pattern:

1. Speed to lead improves from minutes/hours to seconds
2. Follow-up compliance jumps from 20-30% to 100%
3. Database reactivation produces "free" leads
4. No-show rates drop 40-50%
5. Close rates improve because reps get better-qualified leads
6. Headcount requirements drop while output increases
7. CPA drops 35-50%
8. Revenue increases 65-567%

The specific numbers vary by industry and starting point. The direction never varies. It always goes up. Always.

**Six companies. Six industries. One pattern. Revenue up. Costs down. Same leads. Same market. One change: AI. The numbers don't have an opinion. They just tell the truth.**
